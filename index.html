Welcome To My Website!

I am researcher in the area of machine learning and artificial intelligence. In the past two years, I have worked at Samsung Semiconductor Inc. as a Senior Research Scientist. My tasks included researching different computational aspects of recommendation systems and NLP models.

Prior to that, I did my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Minnesota. My Ph.D. research received international awards including the Meta fellowship, where I was one of the 17 winners among more than 800 worldwide Ph.D. applicants, and also an IEEE best paper award.
